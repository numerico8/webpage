About Pimpineo

Pimpineo is a mobile application created to make the international recharge of cellphone balance process very easy for our users. We also have a referral program where our users receive credits for recharges from those they refer. We are a small team directly focused on customer service because they are the most important thing for us. We offer support where you can contact us 24 hours, just fill out the following form by clicking here and one of our representatives will call you back, you can also contact us through our social networks linked at the bottom of the page.
